Brand Analytics (for beginners)

Marketing Anlaytics

Monitoring audience behaviour on social media has so many benefits. For instance, it can help you identify what your target audience likes, what their preferences are, and what they might be interested in hearing more about. It can also show you who your competitors are and how well they are doing compared to you.

This app uses the standard twitter API. The standard API only allows you to retrieve tweets up to 7 days ago and is limited to scraping 18,000 tweets per a 15 minute window.

A Twitter Sentiment analysis project which will scrap twitter for the topic selected by the user. The extracted tweets will then be used to determine the Sentiments of those tweets. The different Visualizations will help us get a feel of the overall mood of the people on Twitter.
